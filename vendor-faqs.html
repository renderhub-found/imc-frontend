Vendor FAQs

Common questions about selling on Inside My Campus.

How much does it cost to register as a vendor?

There's a one-time vendor registration fee, paid securely via Paystack during signup. The exact amount is shown on the vendor registration page.

How do customers pay me?

Most vendors arrange payment directly with customers, usually over WhatsApp, after a customer finds them on the platform.

How long does approval take?

Vendor applications are typically reviewed and approved within 24 hours.

Can I update my products after registering?

Yes — once approved, you can add, edit, or remove products anytime from your vendor dashboard.
