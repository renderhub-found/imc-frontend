Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Inside My Campus ("we", "us") collects information you provide directly (name, email, phone, WhatsApp number, business details) when you register, post a product, event, ad, or apply as a vendor or ambassador.

What we collect

Account details, transaction records via Paystack, content you submit (products, events, news, ads), and basic usage data.

How we use it

To operate the marketplace, process payments, send transactional emails, and improve the platform. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Sharing

Payment details are processed by Paystack under their own privacy policy. Vendor contact details (WhatsApp) are shown publicly on your storefront by design.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your account data by contacting us via the Contact page.
