Our Story

How Inside My Campus started, and where we're headed.

Inside My Campus started with a simple observation: Nigerian campuses are full of student vendors, hustlers, and small businesses that struggle to reach the people who'd actually buy from them — their fellow students.

We built a platform where students can discover vendors, lodges, news, and events happening on their own campus, while giving student entrepreneurs an easy way to get found and get paid.

We're just getting started — more campuses, more features, and more ways for students to earn and connect are on the way.
